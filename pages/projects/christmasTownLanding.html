Web Commercial and Banner

One of the many great design courses I took at Wake Tech helped me learn some great Adobe skills. To use those skills, the students were asked to create a commercial video for Community Navigators Community Builders, a local non-profit that helps local youth learn how to build themselves and their communities through becoming entrepreneurs. They host a fundraiser event every December and needed a video they could share on their website and across social media to encourage attendance.

Among all of my gifted classmates, my design was selected, and they also asked for a web banner/digital flyer so they could print/post the details far and wide. I was honored to be able to create a useful tool to help such a fine organization in my community.
