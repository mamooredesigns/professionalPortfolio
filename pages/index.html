About Me

Salutations! My name is Mary Alice Moore and I am a web designer and front-end developer.

I graduate from Wake Technical Community College in Raleigh, NC in May 2021 with a Associate Degree in Web Design. I have already obtained certificates in development and design from my college already and have worked with Code The Dream for a little over a year to further my skills in front-end development.

I am extremely well versed in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Additional programs, frameworks, and languages I have worked with include WordPress, Dreamweaver, React, and Python to name a few. I have extensively studied Adobe programs to the point of being able to host a continuing education session with my previous employer in Photoshop specifically.

Besides my coding knowledge, I bring some unique skills to the table as well. I have a Bachelor Degree in Psychology which gives me an advantage in understanding and developing User Interfaces, and I have a background in music which gives me a creative edge. If you would like more details on my experience, please continue reading on my About page. I look forward to coding something for you soon!
